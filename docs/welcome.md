# Welcome to Coil

**Earn yield on every limit order. Automatically.**

Coil is a DeFi trading platform on Solana that makes your idle capital work while you wait for limit orders to fill. Instead of your USDC sitting doing nothing, Coil deposits it into Jupiter Lend to earn yield — then auto-executes your order when the price hits.

## The Problem

Every time you place a limit order, your capital sits idle. On centralized exchanges, on Jupiter, everywhere. That's dead money earning nothing while you wait hours, days, or weeks for your price.

## The Solution

Coil deposits your capital into Jupiter Lend the moment you place an order. Your money earns 3-8% APY while waiting. When the price approaches your target, Coil automatically withdraws and places your order on Jupiter Trigger for 24/7 execution.

**You earn yield on capital that would otherwise be idle. Every single order.**

## Key Features

- **Yield on limit orders** — Earn while you wait, automatically
- **OTOCO orders** — Entry + Take Profit + Stop Loss in one
- **DCA with yield** — Dollar-cost average while idle slices earn
- **Compound yield** — Option to add earned yield to your buy size
- **Non-custodial** — Your funds, your wallet, your keys
- **24/7 execution** — Jupiter Trigger fills orders even when you're offline
